⏰ SMART TIMESTAMPS:

< 1 min    → "Just now"
< 60 min   → "5m ago"
< 24 hours → "3h ago"
1 day      → "Yesterday"
< 7 days   → "2d ago"
> 7 days   → "Oct 12"
